Practical support for busy owners

Owning a yacht in the Pacific Northwest is rewarding, but it takes consistent attention.

When boats sit unused, small issues can become expensive ones:

  • Low batteries
  • Bilge or shore power problems
  • Stale systems
  • Inactive engines and generators
  • Weather-related wear
  • Pre-trip surprises

We help keep your boat watched, exercised, and ready.
